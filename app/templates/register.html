What happens after you register
Behavioral Biometrics
Your account is paired with a behavioral profile that captures how you naturally interact, not just what you know.
Continuous Authentication
Once established, your baseline is checked continuously during future sessions — not just at login.
Adaptive Fraud Detection
Deviations from your baseline are surfaced as risk signals, helping flag account takeover attempts early.
Behavioral Trust Scoring
A trust score reflects how closely current activity matches your established behavioral baseline.
Your behavioral profile is not created instantly. It is learned gradually as you log in and use TrustAuth normally.
Privacy & Security Notice
Behavioral typing characteristics are securely processed and never shared.
No biometric images, such as fingerprints or facial scans, are ever stored.
Behavioral data is used solely for authentication and account protection.
This data helps detect account takeover attempts against your account.
